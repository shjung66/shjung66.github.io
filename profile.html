Biography

Sung Hoon Jung is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Physical Therapy at Baekseok University. His research focuses on exercise therapy, sports medicine, musculoskeletal rehabilitation, rehabilitation robotics, digital healthcare, wearable sensors, and AI-based prediction models in physical therapy.

정성훈은 백석대학교 물리치료학과 조교수로 재직 중이며, 운동치료, 스포츠의학, 근골격계 재활, 재활로봇, 디지털 헬스케어, 물리치료 분야의 AI 기반 예측 모델을 중심으로 연구하고 있습니다.
