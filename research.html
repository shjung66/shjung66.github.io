Research

The research program focuses on physical therapy interventions, rehabilitation technologies, and data-informed clinical decision support.

Exercise Therapy and Sports Medicine

Research topics include therapeutic exercise, sports physical therapy, movement analysis, and intervention strategies for athletes and clinical populations.

  • Exercise Therapy
  • Sports Medicine
  • Taekwondo movement analysis

Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation

Research focuses on musculoskeletal pain, functional movement, gait, and rehabilitation strategies for improving physical performance and recovery.

  • Musculoskeletal Rehabilitation
  • Low back pain
  • Gait and movement function

Rehabilitation Robotics and Digital Healthcare

Research interests include rehabilitation robotics, wearable sensors, digital healthcare technologies, and AI-based prediction models in physical therapy.

  • Rehabilitation Robotics
  • Wearable Sensors
  • AI-based Prediction Models in Physical Therapy
